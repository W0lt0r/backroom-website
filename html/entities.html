ENTITiES

Entity 0

Entity0 is often considered the gateway entity, appearing in the basic yellow maze-like rooms that make up the initial layers of the Backrooms. It manifests as a subtle hum emanating from the discolored walls, instilling unease in those who hear it.

Entity 15

A disembodied sound resembling teeth chattering, often accompanied by a drop in temperature. Physical manifestations are rare but include the sensation of something brushing against you.

Entity 1

Also known as "The Wanderer," Entity1 appears as a shadowy figure in the distance. Encounters are rare but often involve a feeling of being watched, and sometimes the distant sound of footsteps that never seem to grow closer.

Entity 2

The Smiler usually appears as a humanoid figure with grotesquely exaggerated facial features, most notably an unnaturally wide, almost ear-to-ear grin filled with razor-sharp teeth. Its eyes are often described as too large for its face and unblinking, adding to its eerie demeanor.

Entity 3

Also known as "The Whisperer," this entity communicates through incomprehensible whispers that echo throughout the rooms. Those who hear the whispers report feeling an irresistible urge to follow the voice, often leading them deeper into the maze.

Entity 20

The Puppeteer, this entity can manipulate objects in the environment and even control less powerful entities. It's thought to be one of the more intelligent beings in the Backrooms.

Entity 8

Known as "The Timekeeper," Entity8 affects how individuals perceive time. In its presence, seconds feel like hours, and time can appear to stop altogether, making navigation and escape extremely challenging.

Entity 6

Entity6, also known as "The Beast," is one of the more aggressive entities. It manifests as a large, indistinct creature that chases after intruders at high speeds. Few have escaped an encounter with Entity6.

Entity 11

The Faceless Man is a humanoid figure lacking facial features. It's commonly reported that staring at this entity for too long can cause psychological distress.

Entity 5

This entity takes the form of an alarmingly regular human being. Referred to as "The Guide," it offers to lead explorers to safety, but those who follow it often find themselves lost.
