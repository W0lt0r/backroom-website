Entity0

Entity0 is the most basic level of the Backrooms. It is often described as a seemingly endless maze of yellow, windowless rooms with old, discolored wallpaper and \ a nauseating smell of moist carpet. Time seems to flow differently here, disorienting newcomers. The air is heavy, and the fluorescent lights above flicker intermittently.

Entity1

In contrast, Entity1 is far more unsettling. It introduces environmental hazards such as rooms that are perpetually on fire or covered in a mysterious fluid. Some explorers have reported hearing distant screams and footsteps that seem to be getting closer but never actually arrive.
